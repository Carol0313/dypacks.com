## 4. LinkedIn Article — 直接发布

```
Title: 3 Mistakes Brands Make When Ordering Custom Packaging from China

I’ve spent the last [X] years helping brands source custom packaging from China. 
Here are the three most common mistakes I see — and how to avoid them.

1. Focusing only on unit price, not landed cost
   Shipping, duties, and quality failures can erase a low unit price quickly.

2. Skipping the sample stage
   Always approve a physical sample before mass production. Colors, materials, 
   and structural fit can differ from digital mockups.

3. Ignoring MOQ flexibility
   Some manufacturers claim high MOQs because they don’t want small orders. 
   Look for partners who offer low-MOQ sampling and scaling.

At DY Packs, we help brands avoid these pitfalls with transparent quoting, 
sample approvals, and flexible order quantities.
```

## 发送建议

1. **邮件发送时间**：周二至周四上午 9-11 点（美国东部时间）
2. **跟进**：3-5 天后发送一次跟进邮件
3. **个性化**：每封邮件至少提到对方网站的一个具体页面或文章
4. **不要群发**：每封邮件单独发送，避免被标记为垃圾邮件
5. **邮箱签名**：包含姓名、职位、公司、官网、电话
